CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • Ibarra is a wealthy young man.
  • Though he was still cultured and well-respected.
  • He is mixed of spanish and filipino.
Maria Clara Delos Santos y Alba
  • Maria Clara is a graceful and demure woman.
  • She is known as the daughter of Kapitan Tiago.
Elias
  • Elias was a selfless man bound by a cody of honor.
  • He was describe as a tall and robust man with a dark complexion.
Don Santiago Delos Santos (Kapitan Tiago)
  • Kapitan Tiago is the legal father of Maria Clara.
  • He is a Filipino socialite and well-respected member of the country's wealthy elite.
Damaso Verdolagas (Padre Damaso)
  • Padre Damaso is a Franciscan friar.
  • He was the former parish curate of San diego.
Bernardo Salvi (Padre Salvi)
  • Padre Salvi is a Spanish friar who arrives in the Philippines to serve in the Catholic Church.
  • He is depicted as a manipulative and ambitious character.
Don Rafael Ibarra
  • Don rafael is the father of Crisostomo Ibarra.
  • He is a rich and brave man who was envied by Padre Damaso due to his wealth.
  • Was also accused of being "heretic".
Pilosopo Tasyo o Don Anastacio
  • Tasio grew up a talented young man, becoming a student of philosophy.
  • He was an intelligent and sagacious individual.
Narcisa (Sisa)
  • Sisa is the mother of Basilio and Crispin.
  • In Noli Me Tangere, she is described as a poor, distressed woman who has suffered greatly.
Crispin
  • Crispin, born the son of Sisa.
  • He and his brother, Basilio, eventually became sacristans for the San Diego Church.
  • Crispin was described as a clever boy, making up riddles.
Basilio
  • Basilio was a young boy living in San Diego and the older brother of Crispin.
  • He was an optimistic and hopeful individual in spite of the hardships he faced.
